About Me

Regardless of how people communicate with each other, there is always a story present. And throught these stories we learn about each other and share a little bit of who we are. To be able to access others in meaningful ways, we need to be open to listen to those stories, even when they are not obvious or explicit. As a Full-Stack Web Developer and a linguistic, I seek to integrate in every website I build my understanding of communication and empathy in human interaction in order to deliver a more engaging web user experience.

My background in qualitative research has taught me the importance of organization and planning before embarking in any project, no matter how big or how little. And a properly planned website can avoid countless hours of debugging later on during development.

Through a full-time intensive 12-week web development bootcamp at UCLA Extension, I gained experience in HTML, CSS, BootStrap, Javascript, Handlebars, Node.Js, Express, MySQL, Sequelize, MongoDB, and Mongoose, and I have since developed a few full-stack MERN apps.
